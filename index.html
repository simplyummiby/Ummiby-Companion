Reading Journey

Read through the entire Qur'an in approximately 294 thoughtfully organized Reading Units.

Reading Units are carefully selected stopping points throughout the Qur'an. Each unit follows the original order of the Qur'an and ends at natural transitions in the discourse, allowing related passages to be read together.

The Qur'anic text itself is never changed—every ayah remains exactly as it appears in the Mushaf.
