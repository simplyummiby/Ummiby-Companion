# Design Philosophy

## Core Principles

- Calm over clutter.
- Gentle encouragement instead of pressure.
- Support both recitation and study without forcing either.
- Resources appear in context.
- Preserve the authenticity of the Qur'an and verified duaas.

## Quran

Reading and study belong together in the Reading Workspace.

## Duaa

Collections are optimized for recitation.

Focus Mode is optimized for reflection, study, and memorization.

## Icons versus Artwork

Use SVG for functional interface icons. Use image files for banners, collection illustrations, and other pictorial artwork. Unicode characters must not substitute for interface icons. Discovery-oriented collection cards may center short content, while progress-oriented Daily Companion cards retain their functional layout.
