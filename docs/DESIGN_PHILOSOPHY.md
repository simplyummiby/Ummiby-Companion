# Design Philosophy

## Core Principles

- Calm over clutter.
- Gentle encouragement instead of pressure.
- Support both recitation and study without forcing either.
- Resources appear in context.
- Preserve the authenticity of the Qur'an and verified duaas.

## Quran

Reading and study belong together in the Reading Workspace.

## Duaa

Collections are optimized for recitation.

Focus Mode is optimized for reflection, study, and memorization.
