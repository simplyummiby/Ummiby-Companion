# Vision

Build a calm, authentic Islamic companion centered on two dedicated workspaces:

- Quran
- Duaa

The application should help users both practice consistently and study deeply without pressure.

## Guiding Vision

- One shared Home introduces the two workspaces.
- Quran and Duaa each have their own Home (dashboard).
- Reading and study should feel intentional rather than overwhelming.
- Authenticity, clarity, and simplicity take precedence over feature count.
