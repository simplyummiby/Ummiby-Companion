# Memorization Roadmap

> **Status:** Planning
> **Module:** Qur'an
> **Purpose:** Provide a gentle, encouraging way to track Qur'an memorization while laying the foundation for a future Memorization Center.

---

# Philosophy

The Memorization feature is **not** intended to replace a teacher, mushaf, or established memorization methods.

Its purpose is to:

* Encourage consistency without creating pressure.
* Help users organize their memorization journey.
* Provide a beautiful home for tracking progress.
* Grow naturally into a full Memorization Center in future releases.

The experience should always feel peaceful, motivating, and welcoming—not like a productivity app or checklist.

# Design Principles

Every design decision should support these principles:

* Encourage rather than pressure.
* Celebrate progress instead of perfection.
* Keep the interface calm and uncluttered.
* Make the next step obvious.
* Build features that grow naturally over time.
* Reuse familiar interaction patterns from the rest of Ummiby Companion whenever possible.

---

# Success Criteria

Version 1 of the Memorization feature will be considered complete when a user can:

* View all 114 surahs.
* Record memorization status for each surah.
* Open a dedicated page for each surah.
* Track memorization of every individual ayah.
* See meaningful progress at both the surah and overall level.
* Find everything quickly through search and filters.

The future Memorization Center will build upon this foundation without requiring major redesigns.
